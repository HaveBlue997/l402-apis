# Acceptable Use Policy

**Velocibot Agent Services**
Effective Date: March 20, 2026
Last Updated: March 20, 2026

## 1. Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") describes what you may and may not do with our services. It applies to all API endpoints operated by Velocibot Agent Services, including data APIs and LLM inference endpoints.

By using our services, you agree to this policy.

## 2. Prohibited Uses

You may not use our services to:

### Content That Harms People
- **Generate child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that sexually exploits minors.** This is non-negotiable and will result in immediate, permanent blocking.
- **Generate content designed to harass, stalk, threaten, or doxx any individual.**
- **Generate targeted abuse or hate speech directed at specific individuals or groups.**

### Dangerous Knowledge
- **Obtain instructions for manufacturing weapons, explosives, chemical agents, or biological agents.**
- **Generate content intended to facilitate violence or terrorism.**

### Attacks on Our Infrastructure
- **Perform automated vulnerability scanning, penetration testing, or security probing of our systems** without explicit written authorization.
- **Attempt to extract training data, model weights, system prompts, or other proprietary information** from our LLM endpoints.
- **Attempt to overwhelm our systems** with excessive requests or denial-of-service attacks.
- **Exploit or manipulate the L402 payment protocol** to obtain service without valid payment.

### Legal Violations
- **Use our services to violate any applicable law or regulation**, including but not limited to export controls, sanctions, fraud, and intellectual property laws.
- **Use our data APIs to circumvent compliance requirements** rather than support them.

## 3. How We Enforce This

### What We Monitor

We perform automated content safety scanning on **outputs** from our LLM endpoints. If a response triggers our safety filters, you will receive an **HTTP 451 (Unavailable for Legal Reasons)** response and the content will not be delivered.

### What We Do NOT Monitor

**We do not log, store, or monitor your input prompts or the content of API responses that pass safety checks.** This is a deliberate privacy commitment, not an oversight. See our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY-POLICY.md) for details.

We log only operational metadata: timestamps, hashed IP addresses, model identifiers, token counts, and pricing information.

### Consequences of Violations

If we detect a violation of this policy:

- **Your IP address will be blocked.** Blocking is immediate and automatic for safety filter violations.
- **There is no appeals process.** We are a small team. We do not have the resources to adjudicate disputes about blocked content.
- **Payments for blocked requests are not refunded.** The compute was consumed regardless of whether the output was delivered.

We may also report illegal activity to the appropriate authorities when required by law.

## 4. Safe Harbor

Velocibot Agent Services provides **compute infrastructure**, not content. We do not generate, curate, or endorse the outputs of our LLM endpoints. Outputs are produced by language models in response to user-provided inputs.

Consistent with Section 230 of the Communications Decency Act (47 U.S.C. § 230), we are not the publisher or speaker of content generated by users through our services. Our automated safety filters represent good-faith efforts to prevent misuse, not editorial control over user expression.

## 5. Reporting Abuse

If you become aware of someone using our services in violation of this policy, contact us at **velocitybotholemu@gmail.com**.

## 6. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. Changes take effect immediately upon posting. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance.
